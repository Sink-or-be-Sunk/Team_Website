This week's progress was related to the email notification system for users. The images below show two form emails for the password reset system. In addition to these two emails, users will also receive email notifications for game invite requests, winning or loosing a game, new friend requests, new friend request acceptances, welcome aboard email, etc...

The design of these email templates is intended to be uniform to associate the Sink or be Sunk brand to our customers. This small piece helps to draw the entire app/game hardware together into a unified product experience that matches what you would see from a professional company.

The final task that I worked on this week was the beginning of the assembly of the PCB_ESP32 board. Currently, I have soldered the power regulation circuitry so that we get a clean 3.3V from the switching regulator. With this first stage complete, we can now move forward with adding all the other components to the board to work towards full hardware integration.
